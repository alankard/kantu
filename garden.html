OUR GARDEN

From garden to table

Our garden is a very special part of our home.
We grow organic fruit, veggies and herbs that are used by our chef to create fresh
and delicious dishes.
Carefully tended by our staff, this space was created with the goal of expanding our
passion for organic products and share our love for mother earth and what she gives
us.
In this magical space we teach and give workshops about eco-agriculture and
ayurvedic nutrition.

All around the property you will find colorful flowers and native trees.

The garden can be visited by all guests.
